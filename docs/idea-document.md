# DeliverGuard AI — Idea Document

## Problem Statement

Gig delivery workers (Zomato, Swiggy, Amazon, Zepto) lose income daily due to uncontrollable external disruptions — heavy rain, extreme heat, air pollution, and traffic jams. They have no safety net.

## Solution

DeliverGuard AI is a parametric insurance platform that:
- Monitors real-time weather, AQI, and traffic conditions
- Automatically triggers claims when disruption thresholds are crossed
- Instantly pays out income protection to workers' bank accounts
- Uses AI to predict risk and recommend optimal premiums

## Key Differentiators

- **Parametric**: No manual claim filing — triggers automatically on data
- **AI-powered**: Risk scoring based on zone, weather history, traffic patterns
- **Instant payout**: Razorpay IMPS transfer within minutes
- **Affordable**: Plans starting at ₹15/week

## Target Users

- Delivery partners on Zomato, Swiggy, Amazon, Zepto
- ~12 million gig workers in India

## Business Model

- Weekly premium subscription
- Platform fee on payouts
- Data insights for delivery platforms
